POLICY

Last updated: May 28, 2025

ProdGate ("we", "us", "our") is a waitlist-stage product. This policy explains what data we collect through this website, why we collect it, and your rights over it.

WHAT WE COLLECT

When you submit the waitlist form, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The timestamp of your submission

We do not collect names, payment information, device data, or IP addresses through this form. We do not use cookies or analytics trackers on this site.

WHY WE COLLECT IT

Your email is collected solely to notify you when ProdGate launches. We will send one launch notification email. Any follow-up communications will only occur if you explicitly opt in.

HOW IT IS STORED

Submissions are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the ProdGate team. Google's infrastructure processes the data under their own Privacy Policy. No other third parties have access to your data.

HOW LONG WE KEEP IT

We retain your email until you request deletion, or until 12 months after the product launches — whichever comes first. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

YOUR RIGHTS

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion — request that we delete your email from our records
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent — opt out at any time with no consequences

EU/EEA residents: these rights are granted under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful basis for processing your data is consent (Article 6(1)(a)).

NO SPAM

We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes. Any email we send will include a clear and functional unsubscribe link in compliance with CAN-SPAM and CASL.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
